# Market Landscape — AI Adoption & Governance (Draft)

## 1) Adoption & Impact Analytics
**Worklytics (MeasureAI)** — adoption dashboard across AI tools (Copilot, Gemini, ChatGPT, Zoom/Slack), usage trends, benchmarks, exportable data. 
Source: https://www.worklytics.co/measureai

**Jellyfish AI Impact** — engineering-focused AI ROI and impact across SDLC; integrates with Jira/GitHub/DevOps tools and AI assistants. 
Source: https://jellyfish.co/platform/jellyfish-ai-impact/

## 2) AI Governance Platforms
**Credo AI** — enterprise AI governance: AI registry, risk intelligence, policy engine, compliance packs (EU AI Act, NIST, etc.). 
Source: https://www.credo.ai/

**Enzai** — AI governance & enablement: intake, registry, compliance, risk monitoring; focuses on legal/compliance enablement. 
Source: https://www.enz.ai/

## 3) Data Governance / Metadata Platforms (adjacent)
**OpenMetadata** — open-source metadata platform for discovery, observability, and governance; can anchor data asset inventory for AI governance. 
Source: https://open-metadata.org/

## 4) LLM Observability (adjacent)
Not direct competitors, but tooling AIPulse may integrate with:
- Langfuse / LangSmith / Langtrace (LLM tracing, evals)
- OpenTelemetry stacks for prompt/response telemetry

---

## Gaps & Opportunity for AIPulse
- **Enablement + governance + adoption** in one workflow (most tools cover only one slice).
- **Agentic ops** to run reporting and monitoring without adding headcount.
- **Use‑case lifecycle** (propose → approve → measure → scale) is still missing in most offerings.
- **Role‑based coaching layer** (1:1 enablement + progress tracking) is typically absent.

## Positioning Suggestions
- “System of record” for AI adoption **and** enablement.
- Focus on **net impact**: time saved vs review cost + rework rate.
- Differentiator: agentic operations for governance and enablement.

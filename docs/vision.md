# Vision

AIPulse helps teams adopt AI **responsibly and measurably**. The goal is to make AI adoption a managed system—so efficiency gains are real and sustained, not offset by hidden review work or operational risk.

## Principles
- **Measure what matters**: impact > activity.
- **Guardrails first**: quality, compliance, and safety are core.
- **Agent‑assisted ops**: agents should do the monitoring and reporting work.
- **Open by default**: documentation and playbooks stay current and public.

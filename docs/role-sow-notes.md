# Role SOW Notes — AI Enablement Lead (Renesas)

## Key Responsibilities (to map into AIPulse modules)
1) **AI Tool Empowerment**
   - Architect/build/maintain AI systems for dev + QA
   - Expand tools to product/content/tech docs, later marketing
   - Rapid PoCs and fast learning loops
   - Continuous scouting of AI developments relevant to stack/use cases

2) **Security & Responsible AI Use**
   - Mitigate AI security risks
   - Promote secure alternatives to ad‑hoc usage
   - Reduce confidential data exposure
   - Enforce appropriate boundaries for AI write‑access

3) **Team Coaching & Adoption**
   - 1:1 coaching per team member
   - Pair programming + peer skill‑sharing
   - Lightweight, visible record of themes/progress

4) **AI Sync / Committee Leadership**
   - Agenda + facilitation
   - Keep leaders aligned
   - Shared summary doc with decisions

5) **Collaboration**
   - Close work with engineering leadership + product/marketing stakeholders

## Operating Principles (product implications)
- Momentum over process
- Execution‑oriented, experiment freely
- Formal project plans are exceptions
